Sightseeing

Yellow Leaf Bay

Beautiful native architecture and white sandy beaches. Yellow Leaf Bay is home to our dock and is a great place to relax.

Boat, Bus, and Helicopter Tours

Guided tours of the island provide an unforgettable and stress-free experience. You can book yours in the booking tab.

Hiking

Nothing eases your mind like a peaceful hike through our rainforest. Be sure to pick up our brochure of hiking trails from our information desks!

Volcano

If you want adventure then look no farther than our active volcano! Perfect for both honeymooners and adventure seekers.

Entertainment

Family Fun

The island has many family friendly activities like zip-lining, an arcade, a movie theater, and bowling.

Museum And Art

Our Local History Museum and art gallaries are perfect for slowing things down and enjoying our culture.

Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine. Some restaurants offer reservations and can be made on the booking tab.

Fishing Tours

Guided fishing tours around the island, available to book in the booking tab!

Grocery Stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Nightlife

Join in on the fun at our Pubs, dance clubs, and our microbreweries.
